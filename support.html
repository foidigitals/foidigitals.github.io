Questions & Help

A short guide to the most common questions. If you do not find what you need, write to us.

01 What is Calorie Reader AI?

Calorie Reader AI is an iOS application that estimates the nutritional content of meals from a single photograph.

02 How does the photo-to-calorie feature work?

The image is transmitted to a vision-capable AI provider that identifies the foods present and estimates portion sizes.

03 How accurate are the estimates?

Photo-based estimates are approximate.

For best results, photograph each meal from above with the full plate visible.

04 Is the AI coach giving me medical advice?

No. The AI nutrition coach is an informational tool.

05 How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Calorie Reader Pro subscriptions are processed by Apple.

On your device, go to Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions.

06 How do I delete my account?

Open Profile → Settings → Delete Account and confirm.

Or write to foidigitals@gmail.com.

07 Why does the app ask for camera permissions?

The camera permission is required to photograph meals.

08 How is my data protected?

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. See our Privacy Policy.

09 How do I contact support?

Write to foidigitals@gmail.com.
