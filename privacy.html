Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information Foi Digitals collects when you use our applications, why we collect it, and the choices you have.

Overview

Foi Digitals operates the “Calorie Reader AI” mobile application and any related services.

By using the Service, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

Account information

When you create an account, we collect your email address.

Profile & nutrition data

To personalize coaching, the Service stores the profile inputs you provide.

Meal photos

When you photograph a meal, the image is transmitted to our backend.

Apple Health data

If you grant permission, the Service can read selected Health data.

Subscription data

Subscriptions are processed by Apple.

Usage analytics & diagnostics

We collect anonymized usage events.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the Service.
  • Personalize coaching and recommendations.
  • Manage your subscription and entitlements.
  • Send transactional notifications you opt into.
  • Diagnose issues and improve the Service.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your meal photos to train any general-purpose model.

Third-Party Services

We rely on a small set of vetted providers:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, storage.
  • A third-party AI provider — receives meal photos for nutrition analysis.
  • RevenueCat — subscription validation and entitlement management.
  • Apple and Google — only the data you authorize is shared.
  • Anonymized analytics and crash reporting providers.

Data Retention

We retain account information for as long as your account is active.

Your Rights

You may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the information we hold about you.
  • Correction — fix inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion — request that we delete your account and data.
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection / restriction — object to certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, write to foidigitals@gmail.com.

Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than the one in which you reside.

Security

We use encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, and least privilege.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time.

Contact

Questions? Write to foidigitals@gmail.com.
