Calorie Reader AI — Privacy

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Calorie Reader AI mobile application.

Overview

Calorie Reader AI is a nutrition tracking application published by Foi Digitals.

Information We Collect

Account information

When you create an account, we collect your email address.

Profile & nutrition data

To personalize coaching, we store the profile inputs you provide.

Meal photos

When you photograph a meal, the image is transmitted to our backend.

Apple Health data

If you grant permission, the app can read selected Health data.

Subscription data

Subscriptions are processed by Apple.

Usage analytics & diagnostics

We collect anonymized usage events and crash diagnostics.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the app.
  • Personalize coaching and recommendations.
  • Manage your subscription and entitlements.
  • Send transactional notifications you opt into.
  • Diagnose issues and improve the app.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not train AI models on your meal photos.

Third-Party Services

We rely on a small set of vetted providers:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, storage.
  • A third-party AI vision provider — receives meal photos for analysis.
  • RevenueCat — subscription validation.
  • Apple and Google — only data you authorize.
  • Anonymized analytics and crash reporting.

Data Retention

We retain account information for as long as your account is active.

Your Rights

You may have the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of your information.
  • Correction — fix inaccurate information.
  • Deletion — request account and data deletion.
  • Portability — receive data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection or restriction of certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, write to foidigitals@gmail.com.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than the one in which you reside.

Security

We use encryption in transit and at rest.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy.

Contact

Questions? Write to foidigitals@gmail.com.
