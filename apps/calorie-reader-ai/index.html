02what it does

designed around real meals.

Snap & log.

Photograph a plate. The model recognizes ingredients and portion sizes, then returns full macros within seconds.

Multi-item meals.

Handles complex plates — multiple dishes, sides, sauces — and breaks down nutrition per component.

Edit if needed.

Disagree with an estimate? Adjust portions or items inline. Your edits help refine the next estimate.

Six languages.

Localised meal recognition for English, Turkish, German, Spanish, French, and Italian — and growing.

Calm interface.

No streaks, no shame loops, no manufactured anxiety. Track when you want, ignore when you don't.

Privacy-first.

Photos are processed and discarded — we don't keep a library of your meals on our servers.
